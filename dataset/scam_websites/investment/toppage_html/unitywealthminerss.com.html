Unity Investment is a popular cryptocurrency exchange that launched in 2017. Its explosive growth is attributed to the combination of low trading fees and fast transactions. We are a group of entrepreneurs, traders, financiers, analysts, dedicated developers and blockchain experts who believe in the upcoming paradigm shift that cryptocurrency and blockchain bring dividends to the global financial industry.


Unity Investment is an extremely popular cryptocurrency exchange and token. Its success is fueled by speed, low fees, and sheer number of transactions. If a lot of people are using a platform, that means it’s easy to find buyers and sellers for the currency you’re after - known as liquidity.


Unity Investment offers its traders a vast number of technical indicators that allowthem to analyse and predict cryptocurrency prices. Our traders work with brokerage companies that offer low spreads, convenient leverage, quick order execution, advanced software, and automated trading capabilities. We provide them with trading advisors, analytical support, and all necessary tools which helps them achieve great results at the exchange and promotes capital gains and the multiplication of capitals of our investors.


Unity Coin (BNB) is an ERC-20 token built on Ethereum. Unity issued 20 million Unity Coins at its ICO, and will eventually buy back half of them and destroy them.
