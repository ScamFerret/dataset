Transaction Transparency

At Capital X Limited, we believe in providing our clients with complete transparency when it comes to their deposit and withdrawal transactions. We understand that our clients want to know exactly where their money is going, and we are committed to providing them with all of the information they need to make informed investment decisions.

When a client makes a deposit into their Capital X Limited account, they can easily track the progress of their deposit through our secure online portal. Clients can see the status of their deposit, including when it was initiated, when it was received by our team, and when it was credited to their account.

Similarly, when a client requests a withdrawal from their Capital X Limited account, they can track the progress of their withdrawal through our online portal. Clients can see the status of their withdrawal, including when it was requested, when it was approved by our team, and when it was sent to their designated withdrawal account.

At every step of the process, we strive to provide our clients with the information they need to feel confident that their funds are being handled securely and transparently. We are committed to maintaining the highest levels of transparency and accountability in all of our deposit and withdrawal transactions, so that our clients can invest with confidence and peace of mind.
