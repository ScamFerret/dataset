About Us - 3H

Your Trusted Source for Pure Palm and Coconut Oils

PT TIGAHA is supplier and exporter of raw materials used in diversified applications such as Soap Making, Biodiesel, Animal Feed, Cooking Oil.

We have a wide range of palm oil by-products such as Mixture of Fatty Acids, PAO Brown, Palm Acid Oil, Hard Residue, Fatty Matter, Lauric Oil, White Oil. Moreover, we are also exporting Coconut Fatty Acid Distillate and RBD Coconut Oil.

We have exported our products to Africa, Asia, Europe and currently have a strong international presence where we are regularly supplying our products.

At PT TIGAHA, we are your trusted supplier and exporter of premium raw materials for a wide range of applications, including Soap Making, Biodiesel Production, Animal Feed, and Cooking Oil.
