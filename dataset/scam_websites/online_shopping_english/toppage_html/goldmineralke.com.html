OUR COMPANY

At Gold and Mineral Merchants Kenya we are group of miners producing unrefined Gold in the form of dust, Bars and Nuggets as well as raw uncut diamonds. We would like to use this medium to inform you all, that we control the mines from which these precious commodities are gotten.

We are based in Nairobi Kenya, Gold and Mineral Merchants Kenya is an indigenous artisanal mining company registered and operating since 1998 and specializes in mining of non-ferrous metal like gold and dealing primarily in gold dust and gold bars. We own and control several mining sites in mining communities of the Eastern and Central Africa.

WHO WE ARE

Before the discovery of six new gold fields where in Kenya in 2012, there were only nine primary gold mining fields in Kenya that were big enough to be nationally considered gold fields and Gold and Mineral Merchants Kenya alone owned four of them. Being one of the prestigious mining companies in Kenya to fully participate in the views and actions of the Artisan Mining Support and Promotion Framework (CAPAM), an outfit created by government to channel gold into the mainstream of industrial production, Gold and Mineral Merchants Kenya held a high priority position in the scramble of those newly discovered gold fields and finally acquired unrestricted licenses to three, raising the number of her gold mining fields to seven.
