Specializing in Natural Fiber Nonwoven (Non Woven) Fabrics and Materials

US Felt is an innovative manufacturer and fabricator of nonwoven (non woven) fabrics, felt and composite materials. As our commitment to you, our customer, we will strive:

  • To deliver solutions to all your product needs and requirements using world class standards
  • To provide exceptional service unsurpassed in our industry, and
  • To form a sound, enduring relationship by gaining your trust and confidence.

US Felt is pleased to announce we have diversified our business and are able to provide the following services:

  • Thermoplastics Injection Molding & Mold Maintenance
  • Custom Printing
    1. Logo
    2. Embroidery
    3. Pad Printing
    4. Screen Printing
  • Customer Product Storage
    1. In our heated warehouse facilities
  • Product Fulfilment Services
    1. Custom Assembly & Sewing
    2. Quality Inspection
    3. Packaging
    4. Inventory Control
    5. Shipping

Use our website to find information on the product you need and to learn more about our company, or get in touch with us to discover the perfect solution for all your applications through our quality, on-time delivery and exceptional customer service.
