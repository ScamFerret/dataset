About cornish hot tubs

Cornish Hot Tubs are a local family run business who in the past 21 years have grown the company to become the largest and most experienced supplier of Hot Tubs in Cornwall.

Cornish Hot Tubs offer a large range of Hot Tubs and Swim Spas from carefully selected quality manufacturers including  American Whirlpool, and Platinum Spas.

By offering a broad range of quality Hot Tubs we can use our sales teams vast experience to help you select a Hot Tub or Swim Spa which is perfect for you.
We do not pressure you into a purchase we simply offer you choice, advice and great prices on quality products.
