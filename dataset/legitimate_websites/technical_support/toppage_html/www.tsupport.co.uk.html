Formed in 1990, TS is a family-run PCB assembly business based in Warwickshire, in the West Midlands region. We supply businesses locally, in Coventry and across the UK. We specialise in mixed technology, small to medium volume PCB assembly – from prototype to full production, inspection and test. We are a dedicated and experienced team with a focus on quality and attention to detail, and have been a long term trusted supplier in a number of sectors. All our electronic production capacity is in-house.
