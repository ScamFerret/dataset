In this growing digital world , we face a future of immense possibilities. With every passing year, new technologies and services are emerging over the horizon.

At CFSK, we are working to propel Kenya to the digital age by training, supplying of computers and other ICT products as well as providing internet to schools. We realize that the next generation has to be well equipped and conversant with the technology in use now and in the future.
