A Full-service Cummins Dealer

We strive to provide transportation solutions that enrich the lives of people, no matter where you are. We proudly offer Blue Bird School Bus products because, as a company, they have worked hard since 1927 to provide safer, smarter, more economical, easier to operate vehicles.  We have buses for sale with capacity from 4 people to 90 people, so we’re sure to satisfy all your commercial transportation needs. In addition to our new equipment inventory, we keep a large stock of used vehicles for all your large scale transportation needs.

We stock OEM and after-market bus parts and accessories from quality transportation industry vendors to keep your fleet in top condition.  From chassis to electrical parts and accessories, you are covered.

We offer five full service locations in Arkansas, Illinois, Missouri, Tennessee, Kentucky, with a 6th location coming soon in Alabama employing trained ASE Certified technicians providing the best possible service support in a timely manner.

Based in Fenton, Missouri, we are proud to represent manufacturers such as ARBOC Specialty Vehicles, Blue Bird Corporation, BraunAbility, Champion Bus, Diamond, ElDorado, Mobility Trans, Starcraft Bus, Carrier Air Conditioning, Ricon, Q’Straint, Sure-Lok, Freedman Seating, and IMMI Seating. These manufacturers offer products for use in applications from public schools to charter coaches and everything in between, including para-transit vehicles.  We provide up to date requirements of the American with Disabilities Act (ADA) regarding the transportation of handicapped passengers.

We at Central States are active in many industry associations and are committed to providing outstanding products and services to our customers. We focus on fulfilling this mission every day and welcome you to Central States.
