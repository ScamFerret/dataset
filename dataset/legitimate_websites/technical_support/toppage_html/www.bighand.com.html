People Productivity

People are the firm; clients need to know that their legal work delivery is done by the appropriate resource and delivering the right quality, speed and value. We help you to demonstrate exceptional value by ensuring all work is always done by the right person.

Financial Productivity

Financial performance determines long-term success; generating suitable return for services delivered is critical. We enable you to improve margins by tracking law firm financial performance, matter-by-matter, in real time.

Document Productivity

Documents are the final product that firms deliver to clients; their quality reflects brand, reputation, experience and customer success. We allow you to showcase your professionalism by providing tools to create perfect legal documents every time.

Business Productivity

Business development is your future; evidencing the firms experience for current and prospective clients is paramount. We enable you to supercharge business development by collating and harnessing all your great experience into outstanding legal pitches and proposals.
