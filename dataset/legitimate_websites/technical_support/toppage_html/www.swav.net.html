How We Do It

From high resolution video, concert audio and intelligent lighting systems to your day-to-day meeting and presentation needs, our state of the art equipment will bring it home.

AUDIO

WE COORDINATE AND PACKAGE SYSTEMS TO PROVIDE:
  • Line array concert sound
  • Corporate production ballroom presentation
  • Arena / amphitheatre environment
  • Public address / paging accommodations
  • Large, medium, small meeting rooms
  • Portable capabilities

LIGHTING

LET US DESIGN AND PLOT A SYSTEM TO FIT YOUR NEEDS FROM:
  • Conventional theatrical lighting
  • Intelligent lighting
  • Special effects
  • Custom logo display
  • Unique trussing sets

VIDEO

WE MAKE IT VISUALLY HAPPEN THROUGH:
  • Single to multiple camera switching packages
  • Front or rear screen presentations
  • High resolution image magnification & content display
  • Proportional room size fast fold & tripod screen surfaces
  • High lumen projectors for video enhancement
  • Full range of plasma & LCD displays for more intimate groupings

SET DESIGN

WE CAN CREATE THE ATMOSPHERE YOU DESIRE BY:
  • Simple, yet effective custom draping and skirting
  • Scrim and cyc backdrop enhancement
  • Modular hard set designs
  • Custom prop fabrication
  • Single to multiple tier staging
