Dartcom is one of the leading manufacturers and integrators of weather satellite and remote sensing ground stations, with solutions for
X-Band EOS, HRPT, AHRPT, DMSP, EUMETCast, HimawariCast, GOES HRIT,
GK-2A LRIT and GK-2A UHRIT.

News:  Dartcom launches new Metop-SG ready X-Band EOS System — January 17th, 2023
1.5m Marine X/L-Band EOS Systems for Australian and British Polar Research Ships — September 9th, 2021
Dartcom launches UHRIT System for GEO-KOMPSAT-2A (GK-2A) — September 17th, 2020
Dartcom systems helping to detect Amazon wildfires — August 21st, 2019

X-Band EOS

Systems for receiving, processing and displaying X-Band EOS data from Terra/Aqua (MODIS), Suomi-NPP, JPSS, FengYun-3 (FY-3) and Metop-SG polar-orbiting satellites. Land-based and marine options available.

More information...

HRPT, AHRPT and DMSP

Systems for receiving, processing and displaying HRPT, AHRPT and DMSP data from NOAA, Metop and DMSP-5D polar-orbiting satellites. Land-based and marine options available.

More information...

EUMETCast

Systems for receiving, processing and displaying MTG, MSG, Himawari, Electro-L, GOES, SAF and other data from the EUMETCast Europe and Africa services.

More information...

HimawariCast

Systems for receiving, processing and displaying Himawari LRIT/HRIT images and other data from the HimawariCast service.

More information...

GOES HRIT

Systems for receiving and processing HRIT images, NWS charts, EMWIN products and other data from GOES geostationary satellites.

More information...

GK-2A LRIT

Systems for receiving and processing LRIT images and other data from the GEO-KOMPSAT-2A (GK-2A) geostationary satellite.

More information...

GK-2A UHRIT

Systems for receiving and processing ultra-high resolution images from the GEO-KOMPSAT-2A (GK-2A) geostationary satellite.
