This Month's General Meeting:

Meeting Date: Tuesday, April 16, 2024 at 6:30PM

Elevenlabs.io, Clipdrop.co, Wasitai.com and the US Justice’s Department’s antitrust case against Apple

NJMUG’s March meeting will held at the Clifton Allwood Library on Tuesday April 16th at 6:30PM in our old room. While face masks are optional, we still recommend maintaining a safe distance from other participants.

In this month’s Ai installment, Bruce Mittelman will show

Elevenlabs.io which offers text to speech and voice cloning for free. Bruce will also show Clipdrop.co (Swap, Generative Fill, etc.) which helps you create stunning visuals in seconds. Last on the AI front, Bruce will show Wasitai.com which claims to be able to tell you if an images was generated with AI.

Our resident legal expert Alan Walter will walk us through the US Department of Justices antitrust case against Apple.
