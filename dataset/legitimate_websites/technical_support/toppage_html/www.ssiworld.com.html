What We Do

SSI Shredding Systems designs & manufactures 1, 2, 3 and 4-shaft industrial shredders & systems, primary reducers and transfer station compactors. For over 40 years we have manufactured thousands of systems, from the simple to the unique.

We are a group of highly dedicated and talented engineers, designers, and technicians. We seek to provide continuous sustainable value: from the conception of the application to the installation, throughout the life of the product. At SSI we say "the best part of the relationship begins with product delivery".
