2024-2025 Kindergarten Registration Information
 
TV Primary School will begin accepting Kindergarten registrations on March 1, 2024. A child must be 5 years old by August 1, 2024! The registration process begins by completing the enrollment forms online in OneView, then submitting the necessary documents to the school office. Visit the tvtrojans.org website and click on the KINDERGARTEN REGISTRATION button to direct you to the OneView Parent Portal.
 
The following documents are needed to finalize registration:

Birth Certificate
Immunization Record
Custody Papers (if applicable)
Proof of Residency (current utility bill or lease agreement/mortgage statement).

For questions, contact the school office at 330.859.2461 or email Primary School Administrative Assistant Krystal Powell at krystal.powell@tvtrojans.org. 
 
2024-2025 Pre-K Registration Information

 

The new Tusky Valley Elementary School will host a half-day preschool program that offers both a morning and afternoon session during the 2024-2025 school year.

Students must be 4 years of age by August 1 or have been identified with a special need in order to qualify for the program. If you have a child who meets the age requirement, or believe your child has a special need such as speech, learning or motor difficulties, please call Jackie Triplett at 330-308-9939 (ext. 8227) or visit www.ECOESC.org/preschool/ to make an appointment.

Due to many factors that impact the number of children we are able to accept in our preschool program, we are not able to guarantee a classroom spot until after the screening process has been completed. We look forward to meeting you and your future Trojan!

Kapper, Lieser Earn All-Ohio Honors
Congratulations to TVHS senior Jake Kapper and sophomore Trista Lieser as each earned Division III All-Ohioan honors at the state cross country meet.  Kapper placed 27th (out of 186) and Lieser placed 19th (out of 185) to earn the honors.  The boys placed 9th as a team while the girls team came home in 17th place.
 
TV Board of Education Recognized by OSBA
Congratulations to the TVLSD Board of Education on being recognized this evening as a Board of Distinction by the Ohio School Boards Association! Only two school boards were recognized with this award in the entire state, and this is the second time our school board has received this award!   The Ohio Boards of Distinction Award honors school boards that have engaged in activities and modeled behaviors that lead to excellence in local school district governance in support of quality education. The qualifications include requirements for each member of the board to participate in training or service as well as a commitment by the full board to engage in activities such as strategic planning and board self-assessment to help guide the direction of the district.  Pictured is Mrs. Katy Johnson, Vice President Mrs. Amy Burrier, President Mr. Richard Gooding, Mr. Ben Overton, and Mrs. Sally Green.
 
Clements, Myers Write Their Names in TV Football Record Book
Congratulations to Tusky Valley seniors Cole Clements and Virgil Myers as each set school records this past football season. Clements became Tusky Valley's all-time passing leader as he ended his career with 3,777 yards passing the old mark of 3,766 set by 2014 graduate Derek Horstman.  Myers set two school records in his lone season as a Trojan. Myers had 971 yards receiving this season surpassing the previous record of 929 set by Sam Gergley in 2007.  Myers added 8 interceptions on the defensive side eclipsing the previous record of 7 set by Pat Kane in 2007.  
 
Boys' & Girls' Cross Country Headed to State!
Congratulations to both our boys' and girls' cross country teams as both teams have qualified for the state cross country meet this weekend.  The meet will take place Fortress Obetz near Columbus.  The girls will run at 10:00 AM and the boys will run at 10:45 AM. A special congratulations to senior Jake Kapper and sophomore Trista Lieser as they each qualified for the state meet as individuals.  Jake placed 12th in the boys race to qualify and Trista was the individual regional champion!
