Do you have the germ of  an idea for a non-fiction book, but you just don’t know how to get it out of your head? Do you have the finished manuscript in your hands but don’t know how to get it into the hands of readers?  If so, Bonnie Davis, the Non-Fiction Book Coach and Marketing Expert is the person you need.

With 20+ years of experience as a virtual assistant, Bonnie found her passion for book publishing after attending the Savannah Book Festival in 2010. Already a life-long book lover and author of several books herself, Bonnie had the opportunity to meet authors, agents and publishers from all over. She arrived home vowing to use her skills as a virtual assistant and her knowledge of the marketing world to become a book coach.

Here’s what Bonnie and her team of experts can do for you:

  • Help you navigate the world of book publishing and marketing step by step.
  • Assist you with writing and editing your book.
  • Provide help with layout and design. Yes, that includes your book cover.
  • Guide you to a publisher who is perfect for your book.
  • Manage the process of self-publishing as opposed to traditional publishing if that’s the route you choose.

Why should you work with Bonnie?

  • She customizes every project to the individual author and book.
  • She has a strong team of experts that work with her.
  • She is committed to finding and reaching your ideal readers.
  • She offers a full variety of services for authors and writers who want to create, publish and promote.

So if you’d like to work with Bonnie, let’s make this easy. You may simply email her at bonnie@davisvirtualassistance.com and include any information you like about your writing. She’ll be able to respond quickly to let you know how she can help.

OR – and here’s a novel idea – rather than emailing just pick up the phone and call her. Bonnie’s number is 949-395-1896.  If she’s in the office, it will be her pleasure to speak to you personally. If not, please leave a message, and she will make every effort to get back to you quickly.

Bonnie looks forward to hearing from you and to working with you to help you get your book published and in the hands of readers.
