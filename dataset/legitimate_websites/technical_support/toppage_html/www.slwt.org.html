What do we do?

At the heart of what we do is raising money towards funding programmes in Sierra Leone. These are programmes that are run by credible, established partners and are carefully chosen by our team.

Because they are locally run, they already have logistics and administrative overheads in place. This means the money we raise can be channelled directly to specific projects. At SLWT, we provide technical and financial support to projects to make them even more efficient.

Since 1999, SLWT has raised over a million dollars (USD 1,000,000) and has contributed to the lives of over 5,000 children and youths.

How you can help

As a small charity, we rely on the good will of people to support our programmes financially as well as volunteering here in the UK or in Sierra Leone.

If you are interested in volunteering, we need help in Sierra Leone in delivering some of our programmes e.g. holiday camps for children, mentoring and teaching basic skills such as using a computer. In the UK, we need help with fundraising.

If you are interested, please contact us.
