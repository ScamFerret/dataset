Welcome

Automation Support Services

Automation Support Services Pte Ltd (ASS) was founded and registered in Singapore in the year 2005. ASS Provide a range of products, solutions and services to the automation industry throughout South East Asia.

ASS are an ISO45001 and bizSAFE Star certified company. To this end, ASS adhere to the highest safety standards, and ensure that all employees receive the relevant and current HSE training. ASS operate a strict RA/RM program in accordance with the ISO45001 and bizSAFE Star directives.

To provide customers with quality systems and services, ASS have aligned themselves with some key European partners. These partners have a wealth of experience in their respective fields, complying to the highest ISO standards, and allow ASS to offer their customers turnkey automation solutions.
