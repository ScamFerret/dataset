Agent

The agent requires no installation. Simply download it, save it somewhere you can find it, and then run it. You will be given an ID and password to allow for the remote support. Pass these along to us, sit back, and let us fix things up for you.

Host

The host is an installation that stays running on your computer so you can request our assistance without even being at your computer. All you need to do is provide us with your ID and password and to leave the computer on. We'll handle the rest.

Helpdesk

Not all problems need a "drop everything and help" response. Submitting tickets alerts all of us instantly, lets us research and reach out with answers (typically within minutes over email for simple issues), and lets us better plan our visits for optimal efficiency.

So who are we and what do we do?

 

Great question! We are a small, family owned business consisting of a group of very highly skilled, professional, and (we like to believe) personable nerds. Whatever your needs, we can get them done. From wall-mounting a television to full network administration for your business, we handle it all. Even up to and including designing and deploying a war-on-terror network for our government! No scope is too big or too small for us to handle. In a nutshell, if it involves technology, give us a shout. The only real exception is that we do not handle professional coding (cudos to the coders out there but we just aren’t into that).

Our driving ideal is to provide service of such quality that you want to continue using us without the need of binding contracts. We will get to know you and your team on a personal basis in order to provide completely customized and effective support. And you’ll know us. No more random people coming in, doing things without speaking to you, and disappearing again.
