Technology Within Reach

Tech Solutions: Affordable Tech Support for Nonprofit Agencies in Dane County

DANEnet is a nonprofit organization so we understand nonprofits; we hold the best interests of nonprofit organizations in our mission and our practice. Our expert tech staff are committed to providing quality, affordable services and appropriate technology. We don’t always promote the latest and the greatest, but rather we work with clients to find the best solutions to their challenges, needs, and budget. Check out our Tech Solutions page for more detailed info.

Digital Equity Project: Connecting Low-Income Households

We are dedicated to improving social and economic equity by connecting low-income households to affordable internet service and computers, and by providing free computer literacy classes in community centers and libraries. It’s estimated that 14,000 households in Dane County lack reliable connectivity and we’re working to change that! Our Digital Equity Project page is a great resource for more info.

How We Succeed

Awesome Workers

DANEnet has experienced, professional, and awesome tech support staff and dedicated, skilled volunteers who serve on our board and help coordinate our Digital Equity Project. We firmly believe in the social and civic benefits of a strong supportive network, whether it be human or digital! Sharing resources and knowledge is the cornerstone of a community that values equity, empowerment, and justice.
Meet our Crew.

Loyal Clients

Over 100 Dane County nonprofits choose DANEnet as their IT/Tech Support professionals, and we are incredibly grateful for them. Whether they have a large number of on-site staff, or a small team of remote workers, we always do our best to help our clients work efficiently and effectively. We know how to leverage technology to improve your mission’s impact without breaking your budget.
Learn more about becoming one of our clients!

Generous Donors

From businesses and individuals that donate their used laptops to us, to donors that provide much-needed funding for our Digital Equity Project, our community’s generosity helps close the digital divide for hundreds of Dane County families, adults, seniors, and veterans every year. By supporting digital equity, you are making a commitment to better preparing adults our workforce while also improving our local economy, education outcomes, health, safety, and civic/social engagement.

Fantastic Partners

Our work leveraging technology to increase community impact and digital equity would not be possible without our wonderful community partners, including: Madison Public Library, City of Madison, Madison Community Foundation, Cascade Asset Management, and dozens of fellow nonprofit agencies as well as various private donors and community centers. When we collaborate and share resources, we can be better serve ALL community members on the other side of the digital divide.
