WHY CRYPTO NEST?

Founded in 2021, Crypto Nest is proud to be the one-stop-shop for all things Crypto hardware in the UK, Europe and beyond. We work tirelessly to source the best Crypto hardware products from market-leading brands around the world. If you're looking to take your first step into the world of self-custody or looking to add additional hardware to your existing self-custody setup then we have the products for you. We proudly supply products to retail Crypto investors, Crypto enthusiasts and all-around security-conscious individuals around the world. Enabling individuals and businesses to maximise security and minimise threats to their digital assets and digital identity.

At Crypto Nest, we offer the best selection of Cryptocurrency hardware wallets, seed phrase backups, security keys and more from brands all around the world. We also have a free education section on our site which includes detailed product setup guides, a Crypto dictionary and simple breakdowns of complex Crypto topics. So, not only do we supply the products, but we also provide the guidance and education needed to use them safely.

As a company our research never stops, we are always on the lookout for the next best hardware wallet, seed phrase backup or security keys. Since the day we took our first delivery of products we have continued to expand our product range at a rapid rate. All of the products we sell we test ourselves so that we can create up-to-date product guides and provide the highest level of technical support to our customers. We can also advise customers on which product would be best for them based on their specific circumstances. 

You're in safe hands when you shop with Crypto Nest, the UK's leading retailer of Cryptocurrency hardware. Trusted by thousands of customers around the world, it has never been easier to take ownership of your Cryptocurrency.

OUR GUARANTEE

We have absolute confidence in our products, so much so that we guarantee 100% satisfaction with every order. If you don't feel as though your expectations have been met or just change your mind, we offer a full 30-day money-back guarantee.

But it doesn't stop there; we also guarantee the authenticity of all our products. All items are shipped directly from the manufacturers to our secure warehouse facility here in the UK and then straight to our customers around the world. For additional peace of mind, you can always reach out to the manufacturer to confirm product authenticity.
