What is a Bitcoin ATM?

Bitcoin ATM is an automated terminal that allows a customer to exchange cryptocurrencies to USD cash and other fiat currencies (depending on their country and region). Any person can buy Bitcoin using an automatic teller machine - crypto ATM. There are two types of Bitcoin ATMs - the first ones allow you to buy Bitcoins and other cryptocurrencies for cash, and the others (2 way Bitcoin ATMs) offer bi-directional operation, enabling the purchase of Bitcoin as well as the sale of Bitcoin for cash transactions. Bitcoin terminals are similar to regular ATMs but they can exchange crypto currencies thanks to a special software - crypto ATMs software.

Bitcoin ATM kiosks are connected to the Internet, allowing the insertion of cash in exchange for Bitcoin, which is transacted to the account of the customer - crypto wallet. They look similar to regular bank ATMs, but they are not connected to any bank account, all operations are made with Bitcoin crypto wallet or cryptocurrency online exchange account of the person. Usually Bitcoin ATMs are located in stores, cafes, restaurants, gas stations or airports.
