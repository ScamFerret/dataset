THE LAST

REWARD STRUCTURE
REQUIREMENTS

  1. Each holder must hold a minimum of 1,000,000,000 $ZADA to qualify for the LMS.
  2. ZADA holders will only be eligible to receive payment from the Last Man Standing Wallet if you haven’t sold any ZADA tokens for 7 days.
  3. Selling or transferring any of your tokens will prohibit you from receiving rewards for that cycle.

CYCLE

PAYMENT
CYCLE

  1. To participate in the first reward cycle, a snapshot is taken and all participating ZADA holders will be accounted for.
  2. At the end of the week we will take another snapshot which will end the cycle for that period.
  3. Only the remaining participants who didn’t sell or transfer their tokens will receive funds from the Last Man Standing Wallet.
  4. After the rewards have been distributed we will begin the next cycle for the next week. (Continuing the same exact process)
