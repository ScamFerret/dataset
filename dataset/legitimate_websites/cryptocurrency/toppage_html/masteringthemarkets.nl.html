WHO ARE WE?

We are Bodhi, Jules, and Lars. Three 22-year-old friends who decided they wanted to change their lives and financial situation. We weren’t born rich. We grew up in a small town in the Netherlands living ordinary lives. Since we were young, we always dreamed of traveling the world and living the luxury lifestyle. We quickly found out we needed a lot of money for this.

Trading & crypto crossed our way and we started studying this. We found out there is much more behind trading than just buying and selling. We started to dig deeper and deeper and started learning about money and the financial system. The more we learned the more we realized there was so much money in this world we could easily get a small piece of the cake.

“ALL IN”

At an early age we dropped out of college and decided to pursue our dreams of becoming full time day traders. After this we quickly saw progress and we started building our portfolios at a rapid speed.We got a lot of negative feedback from our teachers, friends, and family. Because they thought it was too risky and we should just take the safe road with a steady job. We said fuck that we want more out of this life and decided not to listen to them. One of the best decisions we ever made is to not let other people talk us out of our dreams.

We are dream chasers, becoming better everyday and are reaching for our fullest potential. That is why we created Mastering The Markets. A community full of traders who are reaching for their highest potential. Life is to beautiful to not get after it.
