    F.A.Q

    How to refund funds from a virtual card?

    Unfortunately, it is not possible to simply transfer money from a virtual card. To withdraw funds, you need to remove the card. The money will be automatically transferred to the USD wallet within 20 minutes.

    How to top up the balance in USDT?

    To top up your USDT balance, you need to follow these steps: Wallet - Top-up - USDT - select the payment system, and the bot will provide you with a permanent wallet address, to which you can send USDT.

    What needs to be done to issue a virtual card?

    To issue a virtual dollar card, you need to undergo simplified verification. The verification can be done using a passport, international passport, driver's license, residence permit, or ID card. There should be at least $27 in any currency on your Cashinout balance. When issuing a virtual card, $25 will be debited from the Cashinout balance for funding, which is immediately credited to your virtual card. Additionally, 4.5% of this amount or any funding amount of the virtual card will be charged to the benefit of the Cashinout service. The commission is deducted from the bot's general account. You can top up your virtual dollar card from any balance in the Cashinout system in any currency, with automatic currency conversion. Limits for top-up: Visa card - up to $50,000 MasterCard - up to $60,000

    How long does it take for funds to be credited via ?

    Funds via are credited within 10-15 minutes. It is important not to cancel the deposit until the funds are credited to the wallet balance. The transaction will close automatically after the money is credited to the account. Important! When topping up the balance via Faster Payments, it should be done from the card of the verified individual. Otherwise, the funds will not be credited to the account.

    How many virtual cards can be opened?

    There are no specific limitations on the number of virtual cards, however, there are restrictions on the amount that can be loaded onto the card at one time and daily limits. You can find the current limits and fees at the following link: https://cashinout.io/plans
