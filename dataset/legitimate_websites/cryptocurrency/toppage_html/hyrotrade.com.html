About company

HyroTrade, established in 2020, initially offered margin trading on cryptocurrencies like Bitcoin, Ethereum, Litecoin, and more. After three years of working with crypto traders, we recognized the industry's need for more than just a trading platform. Many traders faced a lack of capital, and illegitimate crypto prop trading firms were prevalent.

In response, we shifted our business model and launched HyroTrader in 2023—a prop trading firm dedicated to funding crypto traders. Our aim is to assist traders in obtaining trading capital and offer professional support, helping them thrive in this rapidly evolving industry.
