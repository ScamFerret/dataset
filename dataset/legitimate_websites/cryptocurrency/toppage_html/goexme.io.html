How It Works

1

Select the currency you want to send and the one you want to receive. Our system will immediately find the best exchange rate. Click 'Start Exchange'.

2
Fill and Submit

Enter your receiving address and refund address. Double-check all the information you provide.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does an exchange take?
An exchange takes approximately 15-30 minutes. Sometimes, it may take longer due to network congestion.
Is registration required?
To exchange your funds, registration is not mandatory. However, registering on our service allows you to receive additional bonuses through cashback and a referral program.
My exchange is stuck. What should I do?
Contact our technical support. On the website, you can find all the contact details for our service. Our support team will assist you.
I sent more or less than the specified amount. Will there be a recalculation?
Yes. In both cases, the system will recalculate your request based on the funds received.
