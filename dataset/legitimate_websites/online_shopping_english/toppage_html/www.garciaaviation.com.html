    About Garcia Aviation Co.

    Garcia Aviation Company has been in business for 30 years. We are a full time business. We have supplied the motion picture industry with authentic props and consultation. Some of the productions we have supplied include the movies, The Memphis Belle, HBO Tuskegee Airman, Saving Private Ryan, The Aviator, Amelia Earhart, Band of Brothers, Red Tails, 2019 Midway and several foreign films. We have a superb offering of WWII aircraft flight manuals reprinted from originals in the Garcia Aviation Collection. We have also reproduced many authentic aviation items to include vintage flying helmets, flying scarves, flying gloves, insignia and many other items. We have been a top rated Seller on E-Bay for over 24 years with over 50,000 Positive Feed backs. From the beginning we have always offered a full no hassle money back guarantee. Full satisfaction guarantee. We have shipped thousands of parcels to all corners of the earth. We offer many unique and hard to find aviation items. James Garcia, the owner, travels on a regular basis seeking new aviation treasure from long forgotten warehouses and hard to locate sources. We welcome the opportunity to serve your aviation needs. WE SHIP WORLDWIDE. Your business is truly appreciated. Thanks. James Garcia
