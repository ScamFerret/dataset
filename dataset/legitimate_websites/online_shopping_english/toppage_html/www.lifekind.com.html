About our Certified Organic Mattresses

Lifekind mattresses are certified organic to the Global Organic Textile Standard ("GOTS") - the leading organic standard for textiles. Made by hand in California or Colorado, our mattresses feature a limited 20-year warranty,90-day comfort exchange and many unique design features including dual-comfort options, sculpted surfaces, floating pillow tops, support for adjustable bases and more.

Sleep Organic!®

  • Global Organic Textile Standard ("GOTS") #CU823400

    GOTS is a widely respected certification for organic fibers and textiles, encompassing clothing, bedding, and household goods. The certification process spans the entire supply chain, ensuring stringent standards for sourcing organic raw materials, sustainable production processes, and fair labor practices.

    Annual Certification 

  • Indoor Advantage™ Gold #SCS-IAQ-09458, #SCS-IAQ-09459

    SCS Global Services' Indoor Advantage Gold Certification evaluates indoor air quality of low-emission products, emphasizing manufacturers' commitment to healthier indoor environments. The 'Gold' seal, awarded to top-performing products, transparently communicates superior indoor air quality.

    Annual Certification
