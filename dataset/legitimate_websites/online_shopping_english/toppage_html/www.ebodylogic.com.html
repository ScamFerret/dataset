About Body Logic

We are a locally, family-owned business catering to massage therapists, physical therapists, estheticians, spa businesses, the medical community, as well as many other professions, and individuals, since 1986. Priding ourselves as being coined “The Original Massage Store, since 1986,” we carry high quality massage products and provide personalized service for all our valued customers.

We know there are many other places to shop, so we would like to take this opportunity to prove to you that Body Logic is the only place you need to look for all your massage supply needs.

Please check out Mateo’s Journey. This page is dedicated to my son Mateo, his battle with CHARGE Syndrome, and our family’s path since our lives were changed forever the day he was born.

Each one of you make a difference!! Thank you for the continued loyalty and support!!
