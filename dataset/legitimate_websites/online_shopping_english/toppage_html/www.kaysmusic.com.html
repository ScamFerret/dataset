Welcome to this complete inventory of our online second hand book and music store, based in Brisbane. We specialise in used and out of print sheet music scores, songbooks and method books in all genres and also offer a small selection of second hand non-fiction books of the non-music variety, e.g. Australian History, History, Botany, Gardening, Agriculture, Gemstones, Handcrafts, Art, etc.

Overseas shipping costs are not shown in this link, but buyers in other countries should note that overseas shipping costs are high. See more about how Kay's works

Hint: When searching our catalogues, put titles or names within quotes (" "), or if you are unsure of the correct title, just enter a few key words from the title without quotes to see what is returned in the results: for instance, if you want the sheet music to 'Don't Sit Under The Apple Tree With Anyone Else But Me' you could try entering just some key words from this title: e.g. sit apple tree anyone else. Try to enter specific names, titles or keywords only. Please do not enter terms such as 'looking for' or 'for sale'

We endeavour to update our catalogues frequently, but occasionally a listed item may be unavailable if it has recently been sold
