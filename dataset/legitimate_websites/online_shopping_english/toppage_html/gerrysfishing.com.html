Fishing Tackle – Sold by anglers for anglers

Looking for fishing tackle? Look no further, it’s all here.

Gerry’s Fishing has been an independent, family run fishing tackle store for over 40 years. We have come from a tiny traditional fishing shop into one of the largest stores in the country.

Our Website contains around 30,000 individual products to pick from, yes 30,000 catering for all styles of fishing including carp fishing, sea fishing, coarse fishing and predator fishing. We also have specialist area’s for more bespoke fishing around the UK and abroad.

Our fishing tackle store in the Northwest of England takes some beating. It’s an astonishing 10,000 sqft show room, all on one floor where you can actually drive up, any day of the week and pick up and compare what ever fishing tackle you like.

Lets talk a little about our team here at Gerry’s. We have an expertly crafted team who all have fished since childhood and cover most aspects of fishing so we not only can pick the right products for our ranges but we can offer the advice needed to ensure you pick the right fishing tackle.

For example, we have over 120 beach rods available which is a little daunting at first. That said, Matt is one of our staff who has beach fished since he was young and has a fantastic knowledge of our entire range, we can help you narrow it down to perhaps only a few rods which suit your fishing style, conditions and also price point.
