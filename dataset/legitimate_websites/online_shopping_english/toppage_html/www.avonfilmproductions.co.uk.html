Who are we?

 

Avon Film Productions was founded in Bath in the 1960’s as a film production company, later to become a photographic retailer and distributor. Based in the centre of Bath for more than 50 years we have been serving the people of the South West with our expertise in photography.

In 2007 we closed the city centre shop, moving to our present location on the outskirts of Bath. We retained many of our loyal private customers whilst also increasing the number of schools, colleges, hospitals and universities we support with our knowledge and experience.

We supply all traditional black and white photographic equipment and materials. We are able to advise and supply all requirements for darkroom and studios set ups.

We are also experienced in the installation and supply of CCTV monitoring, sound and picture recording systems. These systems are employed in observation rooms, hospitals and industrial locations.
