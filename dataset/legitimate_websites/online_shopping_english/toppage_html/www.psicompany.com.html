About Puget Sound Instrument

Puget Sound Instrument (PSICOMPANY) is a leading worldwide provider for two-way radio, situational awareness monitoring software and commercial marine products.


Founded in 1965, the company enjoys a seasoned staff of sales and technical experts, with an average tenure of over 10 years that provide highly accurate proposals and best in class technical service.


Operating with the highest integrity is why school districts, police departments, cities, military, healthcare, commercial and other business customers depend on us to deliver critical 24/7 communication, situational awareness monitoring and commercial navigation systems.


PSICOMPANY enjoys a solid reputation among some 500 local and 6000 worldwide customers it serves. The company has earned many awards and elite class dealerships from the manufacturers it represents.


The main office is in Seattle-Tacoma metro area in Washington State on the west coast of the US mainland. The name Puget Sound comes from the hundred-mile-deep in-land sea connected to the Pacific Ocean via the Strait of Juan de Fuca. The company office is about 5 miles from the Port of Tacoma.
