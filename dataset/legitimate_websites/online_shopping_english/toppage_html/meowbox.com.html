What's in a meowbox?

meowbox is a subscription box just for cats, shipping fun toys and yummy treats to your doorstep every month, or bi-monthly. Each item is thoughtfully selected or designed to win over the most finicky feline.

Cat toys

Our whole team of cat lovers lends a paw in designing adorable monthly themes and unique toy ideas. We take pride in featuring products from small businesses in many of your meowboxes.

Cat treats

The tasty, healthy treats in your kitty's meowbox are often made exclusively for meowbox subscribers. We would only give your fur babies what we would give our own.

Simply select the "No Food" option at checkout to substitute treats for an extra toy.

A box!

Many cats would consider the box itself a bonus toy. As the ancient feline purrverb goes: "If I fits, I sits."
