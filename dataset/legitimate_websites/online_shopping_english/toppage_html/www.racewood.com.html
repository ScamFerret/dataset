WHO we are
Racewood is the world's leading designer and manufacturer of Equestrian Simulators. Championed by professional bodies across the globe, the Racewood Simulators have been playing a pivotal role in horse-rider performance for over 30 years, whether at introductory level or professional. 

Always at the forefront of innovation, Racewood continues to develop the most up-to-date technology and software that enables riders to optimise their performance and instructors to offer first-class training to their clients.
