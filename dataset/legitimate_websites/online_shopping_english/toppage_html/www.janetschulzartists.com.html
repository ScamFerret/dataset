Boat Portraits

 

"No one knows the joy of sailing free over the oceans save those who have had the experience"_Joshua Slocum from Sailing Around the World

 

Power, sail, classic woodie or dingy..
it's your special yacht

 

My goal is bring to the minds of the recipient/collector that unique joy known only to boaters by translating it into the highest level of quality art. Tell me what kind of boat you have, the model, and the year and I will place it in the location and time of day you love the most.--J.S.

Endless Possibilities....

 

A garden, a favorite room, a beautiful vista, a human portrait, the bridal party...

 

We capture our special places and memories with our cameras and phones and place the pictures in scrapbooks, on Facebook, or stored on our phones. But, just sometimes wouldn't it be wonderful to display that cherished memory in a work of art, framed and hanging on the wall.
