Nice to Meat You

At Savenors, we are passionate about serving you the best quality meats so you can enjoy a gastronomic experience like no other. From our humanely-raised and pastured lamb, USDA Prime and local beef, Vermont GMO-free/Antibiotic-free Berkshire pork, and organic free-range chicken to our specialty cuts of wild game meats and hard-to-find offals, we take great pride in delivering the most delicious and mouth-watering options to your table. And we're not just talking about locally sourced ingredients - we go above and beyond to ensure that every cut of meat is of the highest quality, so you can taste the difference in every bite. Whether you're a seasoned chef or just getting your feet wet in the kitchen, we've got you covered.
