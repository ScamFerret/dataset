WELCOME!

Thanks for stopping by! Everything on this site is handmade by me. I've been making hippie clothing for about seven years now. All my clothes that are currently for sale, including hippie patchwork dresses, apron tops, skirts and bags can be found in my Closet.

If you can't find what you're looking for, I will be more than happy to make your very own custom piece. If you need some ideas to spark your creativity, just browse around my sold goods in the Gallery.

All of my fabrics are pre-washed, so they're not going to shrink. I take great pride in my art and pay close attention to the finest details. All seams are serged for durability, using a 4-thread overlock stitch.

If you have any questions, please take a look at my FAQs page or Contact Me!

Peace!
Marsha
