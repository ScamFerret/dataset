The Best Amish Made Furnishings - At the Best Prices!

Clayborne’s Amish Furniture of the South Carolina midlands is your long-time family owned source for top quality heirloom Amish crafted furniture, made of solid oak, cherry, maple, hickory and walnut hardwoods, along with rustic cedar, all expertly made by skilled Amish craftsmen!  No cheap pressed-wood furniture will be found here! Over 21 years when we had our physical store, and Still on the web since 2000, Clayborne’s has been providing top quality Amish made solid oak furniture and service to people all over the US for decades!

Our heirloom quality Amish oak furnishings for your dining room, bedroom, kitchen, living room, office and more are solid hardwood, except drawer bottoms, shelf backs, etc, which are typically oak or cherry veneer. We have single and double pedestal dining tables, traditional leg tables, beds, dressers, hutches, chairs, stools, pantry cabinets, entertainment centers, rockers and even toys and office furniture. If you don’t see what you need, please E-mail or give us a call. The Amish furniture builders continuously create new products, so check back often. Our web site represents just a fraction of the items we can get from our Amish heirloom furniture builders.

Generally, for anything you see here, we can have it custom built or finished to your needs. Amish made furniture is usually in either solid oak or cherry, but we also have Amish made items in softer woods like cedar and pine, and other items are available in maple, hickory and walnut.  Choose from light or dark finishes to match your decor - just let us know what you need.  We invite you to take a look around, and we look forward to taking care of all your Amish home furnishing needs.
