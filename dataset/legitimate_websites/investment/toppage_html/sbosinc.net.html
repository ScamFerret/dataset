Securities BackOffice Solutions, Inc. develops and supports securities commission and compliance software for broker dealers, brokerage firms, banks and insurance companies operating in the securities industry.   


SBOS, Inc. was founded in 1997 for the sole purpose of designing a true automated securities commission system for the independent broker dealer. We believe our commission software Securities ProStation® more than meets that objective.  


SBOS offers a web based user interface, Securities AdvisorStation™ that enables your advisors to access their commission information in the Securities ProStation system. Your advisors can easily and quickly get answers 24/7/365 from anywhere.  


Compliance is an integral part of every securities firms business. Securities BackOffice Solutions, Inc. specializes in providing software systems that focus on the role of compliance with securities rules and regulations. Securities SurveillanceStation™ is an automated exception reporting system that was designed based on our staff’s compliance expertise.


Today all three systems are used by small, medium and large broker dealers throughout the United States to maximize back office efficiency and minimize operational and compliance costs. The combined functionality of our products allows you to have a truly integrated suite of software to fully automate your business.
