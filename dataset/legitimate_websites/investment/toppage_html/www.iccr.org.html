Catalyzing Corporate Change

For over five decades, members of ICCR a coalition of over 300 global institutional investors representing more than $4 trillion in managed assets have leveraged their role as shareholders to call for improved performance on critical environmental and social concerns at some of the worlds most powerful companies.

Our Work:

Through collective action, ICCR members make both the moral and business case for improved corporate performance on critical ESG risks, pressing corporations to adopt and implement policies and practices that will mitigate business risk and address adverse environmental and social impacts.

The Climate Crisis

Given the existential threat climate change and pollution pose to the planet, investors expect companies to mitigate their environmental risks.
Advancing Worker Justice

As key corporate stakeholders, investors expect companies to implement policies and practices that support worker welfare.
Health Equity

ICCR members advocate for corporate reforms that increase the equitable access and affordability of health care both in the U.S. and abroad.
Corporate Political Responsibility

ICCR members press for responsible corporate political activities that align with company values and advance democracy.
Equitable Global Supply Chains

Global supply chains are in need of a transformation, one that benefits all stakeholders.

ICCR Membership

Membership in ICCR is open to any institutional investor interested in promoting corporate social responsibility.
