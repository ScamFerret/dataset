Our Product Providers

Our approved product list is drawn from a broad range of fund managers. It includes those funds we believe are the best in each major asset class Australian shares, global shares, listed property, fixed interest and cash. 

It incorporates a core selection of high profile fund managers together with some specialist boutique funds selected to meet specific client needs.  We conduct rigorous analysis to identify the most skilled and consistently high performing fund managers in each asset class and each fund manager is reviewed regularly to ensure it continues to meet our criteria.
