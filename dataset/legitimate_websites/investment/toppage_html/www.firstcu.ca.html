                                                                        A Better Way to Bank

                                                                        Credit unions have a set of co-operative principles and values that guide our actions. Honesty, openness, social responsibility and caring of others are the ethical values that keep us focused on positively impacting our members, employees and communities.

                                                                        First Credit Union is local, but we are also part of a global co-operative movement. Join us, and millions of other people, as we work to create a more equitable, sustainable and resilient world.


                                                                        Key Benefits of Membership

                                                                        Local, friendly full-service banking with competitive rates
                                                                        Support positive change in your community, we reinvest 10% of profits locally
                                                                        Access to the largest network of surcharge-free ATMs in Canada
