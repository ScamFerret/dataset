Who is Cadiz Asset Management?

 

Cadiz Asset Management is a fund manager that specialises in Fixed Income investments.

Cadiz Asset Management was acquired by Spirit Invest in 2018 and has since evolved to be the specialist Fixed Income fund manager within the group that manages assets for both individual and institutional clients.

Cadiz is supported by an experienced team of investment professionals, combined with a tried-and-tested investment process that produces consistent results over time.
