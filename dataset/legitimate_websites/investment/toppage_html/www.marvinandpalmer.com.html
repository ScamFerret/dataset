Meet Our Investment Team

David Marvin

Chairman of the Board, Portfolio Manager

Mr. Marvin is a member of the Management Committee. Together with Stan Palmer, he founded the firm in July 1986. Prior to starting Marvin & Palmer Associates, Mr. Marvin served for ten years as Vice President in charge of the $10 billion internally-managed DuPont Pension Fund. Preceding his employment at DuPont, he worked for Investors Diversified Services as the Head Portfolio Manager for the IDS Stock Fund, the largest equity mutual fund in the United States at the time of his tenure. He started in the investment business in 1965 as a securities analyst for Chicago Title & Trust. Mr. Marvin earned his M.B.A. from Northwestern University and B.S. from the University of Illinois. He is a CFA charterholder and a member of the CFA Institute.

Todd Marvin

President, Portfolio Manager

Mr. Marvin is a member of the Management Committee. He began with the firm as an International Analyst in 1991 and became a Portfolio Manager in 1993. Prior to joining the firm, he worked for Oppenheimer & Company as an analyst in investment banking. Mr. Marvin earned his B.A. from Wesleyan University. He is a CFA charterholder, a member of the CFA Institute and a member of the CFA Society of Philadelphia.

Stephen Marvin

President, Portfolio Manager

Mr. Marvin joined the firm in 1994 as a Global Analyst and became a Portfolio Manager in 1997. Prior to joining the firm, he worked for Bear, Stearns & Company as a Corporate Finance Analyst. Mr. Marvin earned his B.A. from Carleton College. He is a Financial Risk Manager (FRM) – Certified by the Global Association of Risk Professionals.

Jay Middleton

Portfolio Manager

Mr. Middleton joined the firm in 1989 as a Global Analyst and became a Portfolio Manager in 1992. Mr. Middleton earned his B.A. from Wesleyan University. He is a member of the Phi Beta Kappa Honor Society.
