Get Started

  1. To begin, complete our Place Accounts Form or the preliminary Free Evaluation form and submit via email to NJIC; fax your claim to NJIC @ 215-646-9521 or call NJIC to discuss your claim @ 215-646-9520.
  2. After receipt of your claims, NJIC begins the process of verifying the business status of your delinquent customers, determining recoverability, and the appropriate collection strategy.
  3. NJIC will make contact with your customer in pursuit of the monies which are due and owing to your company.
  4. If NJIC recovers the money due, we receive a commission fee based on the age of the oldest invoice: no recovery, no fee.
