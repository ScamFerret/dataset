Thought leaders in the field of securities regulation and zealous advocates for our clients.
Pickard Djinis and Pisarri LLP is a Washington, D.C.-based boutique law firm that has provided a full range of securities regulatory, compliance and enforcement defense services to a wide array of financial industry participants for over thirty years. With a reputation as thought leaders in the field of securities regulation and zealous advocates for our clients, we represent broker-dealers, investment advisers, independent research firms, NRSROs, and providers of financial information and services before the Securities and Exchange Commission, FINRA and other federal, state and self-regulatory bodies. Most of our attorneys were previously associated with the SEC or FINRA/NASD.
