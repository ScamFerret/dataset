Welcome to Latter & Blum | Compass

Latter & Blum | Compass has been helping people buy and sell their most important investments in the Gulf South since 1916. We understand that real estate is more than just a business transaction. Its an essential part of building a safe, happy, and prosperous life.

Yet, its our large network of real estate professionals that really makes the difference.
