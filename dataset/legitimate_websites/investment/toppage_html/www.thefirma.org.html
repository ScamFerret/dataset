Welcome to FIRMA

The Fiduciary and Investment Risk Management Association, Inc. (FIRMA™) is the leading provider of current and relevant fiduciary and investment risk management education and networking opportunities to risk management, compliance, and audit professionals across all lines of defense at banks, trust companies, investment advisors, broker-dealers, insurance companies and other financial service organizations. FIRMA has the objective of providing fiduciary risk managers with the tools and knowledge needed to perform their functions in the rapidly changing financial services landscape.
