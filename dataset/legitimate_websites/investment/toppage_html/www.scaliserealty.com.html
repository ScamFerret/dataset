About Us

A leader in luxury second homes and investment property real estate for almost 30 years.

Scalise Realty is a General Brokerage Real Estate Firm specializing in Resort & Residential Sales along the entire Grand Strand for almost 30 Years!

Scalise Realty is the Exclusive on-site sales agency at North Beach Plantation located in North Myrtle Beach, SC. North Beach Plantation is a 62-acre Oceanfront Destination Resort featuring over 2.5 acres of oceanfront pool amenities, a world-class spa and state-of-the-art health club, 3 restaurants & Banquet Hall.
Ownership opportunities include oceanfront condominiums from 1 to 5 bedrooms, 2 & 3 bedroom townhomes, and single family homes from 2 to 5 bedrooms from which to choose.

Scalise Realty can list or sell properties ANYWHERE along the Grand Strand so call one of our sales professionals for all of your real estate needs. Let one of our team of experts go to work for you today!

Whether purchasing or selling with Scalise Realty you can always count on the most comprehensive attention to detail and excellent service.

We sincerely appreciate our clients business and all of their referrals.
