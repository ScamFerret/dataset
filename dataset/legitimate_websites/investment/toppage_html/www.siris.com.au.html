The Sustainable Investment Research Institute (SIRIS) is a dedicated, specialised ESG research group, providing investment research and analysis of corporate sustainability and governance risks and opportunities.

 

Our research primarily focuses on the Asia-Pacific region with a team that includes multilingual Asian-language speaking analysts that holds extensive knowledge on local sustainability and governance practices. In addition, through our international research partnerships, SIRIS research enables global benchmarking and the development of seamless global research solutions.

 

SIRIS’ services are designed to generate solutions, focusing on capability based service, ranging from data analysis and screening to tailored thematic analysis to meet investment research needs.
 
   
 

Principles for Responsible Investment

SIRIS is a proud member of Principles for Responsible Investment (PRI), a project that aims to help integrate consideration of environmental, social and governance issues by institutional investors into investment decision-making and ownership practices, thereby improving long-term returns to beneficiaries. PRI is an initiative of UNEP Finance Initiative and the UN Global Compact.
