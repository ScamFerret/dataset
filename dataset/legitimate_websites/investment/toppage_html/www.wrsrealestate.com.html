    ABOUT US

    WE KNOW DEVELOPMENT

    WRS Inc. is a full-service commercial real estate development and investment firm that specializes in retail, office, industrial and residential development, property management, commercial brokerage, and construction management. Since the company’s launch in 2001, WRS Inc. has developed more than 45 anchored retail shopping centers and mixed-use projects, comprising more than 10 million square-feet. 

    ​

    WRS Inc. often sources transactions through partnerships with landowners and local developers that need a partner that can bring a full Design-Build-Finance solution to complicated situations. Many of these landowners are community institutions and small local developers. WRS Inc. takes special pride in lending its expertise in these instances and has a proven track record of providing positive environmental and social impacts on communities across the Southeast while providing enhanced returns to its investors.

    ​

    Collectively, our executive team has more than 150 years of experience in the development industry and has
    built strong relationships with both sellers and tenants alike, in the retail, office, industrial and residential product types, allowing more off market opportunities. WRS Inc. provides a variety of brokerage services such as market studies, site selection, contract negotiation, and coordination of zoning and permitting processes. WRS Property Management, LLC focuses on the day-to-day operations of our asset portfolio, leasing and the needs of our tenants. By providing these services in-house, WRS manages and streamlines its development or investment strategies from acquisition to

    stabilization and sale.
