Katzbach Insurance

We are a family-owned and operated agency, established in 1983 by John Katzbach, Jr.   Katzbach Insurance Agency serves the insurance needs of many individuals, families and business concerns in Cuyahoga County and adjacent areas.

 

As an independent agency representing a carefully selected group of financially sound and reputable insurance companies, we place your policy with the company offering the best coverage at a competitive price.

 

 John Katzbach Jr. is the owner of the Katzbach Insurance Agency, Inc. which is a firm of proven professionals and caring conscientious people; the kind of people you can depend on.  We are bound to a high standard of excellence in all that we do, and strive to establish a firm relationship of mutual trust and service with each of our clients.  As a Trusted Choice® agency, we are dedicated to you and are committed to treating you as a person, not a policy.
