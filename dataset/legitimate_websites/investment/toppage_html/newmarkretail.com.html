Cartier

Proclaimed by The Cut to be “quintessentially New York”, this four-story loft with a speakeasy and rooftop terrace embodies the iconic legacy of Cartier with the soul of downtown.

From the beginning, Newmark envisioned this historic space for Cartier—and Cartier only— knowing this opportunity was uniquely suited to the brand’s next chapter.

After 20 years of eyeing Downtown, Cartier’s brand comes to life in this grandiose space, complete with an original intricate door by famous sculptor William Tarr.

SoHo, New York City

📷: Cartier

Chick-fil-A

Your brand has purpose. Your location should too.

Rich with a history of family-driven establishments, this thriving Manhattan neighborhood spot presented the perfect opportunity to connect Upper East Siders with a staple of the South. Enter: Chick-fil-A’s first ever express mobile pickup location.

The 79th & 2nd location is one of two digitally focused concepts from Chick-fil-A, and it neighbors another Newmark partner, Goldfish Swim School. After recent closures along 79th, we knew it was time to usher in a new era of Upper East Side family favorites.

Upper East Side, New York City

📷: Chick-fil-A

Ernesta

When Peloton founder John Foley, was ready to launch his next concept, Ernesta custom rugs, he turned to Newmark. Over the last decade, our innovative team of advisors has helped Peloton emerge as a premier fitness experience by arranging 55 leases across North America, including its HQ in New York City.

As Ernesta, a digitally native brand, made a push to “go brick and mortar,” they turned to Newmark to realize their merchandising vision and open their first-ever showroom in Manhattan.

Manhattan, New York City

📷: Ernesta
