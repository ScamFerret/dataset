We are an independent, privately-held investment bank. Our deep team of experienced investment bankers provide thoughtful, unbiased merger & acquisition, capital raising, valuation, and financial advisory services to privately-held businesses, publicly traded corporations, and capital providers.

We are proud to have successfully advised hundreds of clients over the past 35+ years on some of their most impactful business decisions.

Mergers & Acquisitions

Matrix’s merger and acquisition services include: Company sales and recapitalizations, corporate carve-outs, special situations, management buyouts, buy-side representation and not-for-profit transactions.
