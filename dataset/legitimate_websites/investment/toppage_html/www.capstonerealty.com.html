Welcome to Capstone

Capstone Realty is a full-service real estate company providing professional and attentive leasing, management, design and development services to our clients in Louisville Metro. We are a partnership of experienced real estate professionals active in acquisition, land development, project architecture, leasing, and property management.

As part of our real estate portfolio, our premier developments include Commerce Crossings, 350+ acre business park with space available for a variety of business applications in Louisville, Kentucky and River Ridge Commerce Center in Indiana, where our developments are part of a 6,000-acre multi-use mega site experiencing unprecedented growth, just 10 minutes from downtown Louisville.
