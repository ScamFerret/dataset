The Rosemyr Corporation is an investment company primarily focused on commercial real estate and is based in Henderson, North Carolina. Rosemyr owns and manages more than 2.8 million square feet of commercial and storage properties located throughout North Carolina and Virginia. Our property portfolio includes grocery-anchored shopping centers, single-tenant retail properties, and a small number of industrial, office, and apartment buildings. Via our AA Self Storage division, we own and operate 14 self storage facilities across the two states. Rosemyr also invests in partnership with likeminded companies outside of our core markets. Through our work, we are committed to making a positive contribution to our communities in every interaction, always.
