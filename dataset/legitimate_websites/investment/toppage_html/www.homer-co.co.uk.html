Thank you for visiting our website. We hope you will gain a sense of our strengths, our culture and the value we place on serving our clients and invite you to explore how we may be able to assist you.

We are a highly respected firm of Independent Financial Advisers known for our private client and financial planning work. We aim to excel technically, make complex issues easy to understand and give our clients options they hadn't even thought of. We are passionate about the advice we offer to our clients - we care about what we do and the way that we do it. Homer & Co LLP work in partnership with our clients, and other professional advisers such as solicitors and accountants, to meet their financial aspirations and we recognise the importance of providing long-term on-going assistance and guidance.

If you would like to get in touch, we would be delighted to hear from you.
