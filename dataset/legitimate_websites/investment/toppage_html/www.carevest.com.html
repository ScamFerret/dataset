CareVest Capital Inc.

Real Estate Lending

CareVest® Capital Inc., a member of the CareVest group of companies (CareVest), is a Canadian commercial mortgage broker and lender with offices in Alberta and British Columbia. Starting in Calgary in 1994 and with offices now in Calgary, Vancouver and Victoria, CareVest provides customized mortgage financing solutions to Canada’s real estate industry.

Management Team

Jeevan Khunkhun

President

Mr. Khunkhun is the President and Director of CareVest Capital Inc. Located in our Vancouver office, Mr. Khunkhun is responsible for sourcing, underwriting, negotiating and managing both new mortgage transactions and existing accounts in British Columbia and Alberta. Prior to joining CareVest in 2006, Mr. Khunkhun was a Senior Manager with Commercial Real Estate Division of Royal Bank of Canada Main Centre, Vancouver. Mr. Khunkhun holds a Bachelor of Commerce degree in Finance from the University of British Columbia, and an MBA from the University of Manitoba.

Clinton Wark

Vice President of Lending

Mr. Wark is  Senior Vice-President - Lending and Director of CareVest Capital Inc. Located in our Vancouver office. Mr. Wark is responsible for sourcing, underwriting, negotiating and managing both new mortgage transactions and existing accounts in British Columbia and Alberta. Mr. Wark has over 25 years of experience in the real estate finance industry including direct lending, and mortgage brokerage involving all commercial real estate product types with an emphasis on land development and construction projects. Mr. Wark holds a Bachelor of Commerce (Honors) degree from the University of Manitoba with a major in finance.
